OUR STORY

We take pride in producing mouth-watering hand crafted artisan breads, cakes and pastries freshly baked each morning.Our master bakers have been serving up some incredible baked goods for the past 15 years, these guys are seriously passionate when it comes to baking.

We produce products everyday, here are some of the products we produce:

- Handmade Croissants, Pain au Chocolat, Pain au Raisin

- Our Famous Croissant which is the best in Dublin!

- Muffins, Cookies, Traybakes

- Variety of handmade breads and baguettes

- Celebration & Novelty Cakes

- Tarts & desserts and many more

OUR MISSION

Our aim is to produce handmade artisan breads, cakes and pastries fresh every day. We are one of very few bakeries in Ireland producing handmade pastries including Croissants, Pain au chocolat and Traditional Almond Croissants to name but a few. At Bakelicious, we are committed to using fresh and natural ingredients, and do not use preservatives in our products.
